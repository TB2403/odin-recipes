Spaghetti with Ground Beef

A simple classic spaghetti recipe with ground beef.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 28-ounce canned diced tomatoes
  • 1 16-ounce canned tomato sauce
  • 1 6-ounce canned tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook and stir continuously for 5-7 minutes or until meat is brown and crumbly and the vegetables are soft. Remove the grease.
  2. Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan. Season the sauce with the oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  3. Cook the spaghetti according to the packet instruction and drain.
  4. Add the sauce onto the spaghetti and serve.
