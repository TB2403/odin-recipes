Miso Soup

A simple recipe to make a simple, but great tasting, miso soup.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 teaspoon dashi granules
  • 3 tablespoons miso paste
  • 1 8-ounce package of silken tofu, diced
  • 2 green onions, sliced diagonally into Â½ inch pieces

Steps

  1. Combine the water and the dashi granules in a small pot over medium high, bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat to medium and whisk in the miso paste.
  3. Gently stir in the tofu.
  4. Add a bit of the green onions leaving some as garnish. Serve the soup and garnish with some green onions.
